Hi! I'm a Ph.D. candidate and Fulbright scholar at the College of Media & Communication at Texas Tech University.

I’m interested in the diverse dynamics of communication in everyday politics, with a focus on interpersonal, mass-mediated, and platform-mediated interactions. Currently, I’m working in projects that explore the psychological factors influencing individuals’ decisions to engage in or avoid discussing politics.

I am also a part-time instructor at the College of Media & Communication, and teach courses about business and professional communication.

Before coming to Tech, I completed a master's degree in Social Communication and a bachelor's degree in Journalism at the University of Minas Gerais in Belo Horizonte, Brazil.
